Rock, Paper, Scissors

How to Play

Each round, choose a hand to throw against the computer opponent: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The computer opponent will randomly choose one of the three hands to throw against you.

In the case of equal hands being thrown, the round is a draw, and no points are scored. Otherwise, the winner of the round scores a point. The first to score five points wins the match. Have fun!

Winning Hands

Rock beats Scissors, but loses to Paper. Paper beats Rock, but loses to Scissors. Scissors beats Paper, but loses to Rock. Equal hands draw.
