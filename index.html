Rock, Paper, Scissors

How to Play

Choose a hand to throw against the computer opponent: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. A dialog box will prompt you to type in your chosen hand. The computer opponent will randomly choose one of the three hands to throw against yours. See the results of the game in the browser console.

In the event of a draw, no points are assigned. Otherwise, the winner of the hand scores a point.

Winning Hands

Rock beats Scissors, but loses to Paper; Paper beats Rock, but loses to Scissors; Scissors beats Paper, but loses to Rock. Equal hands draw.
